e-Tour
Tired of wasting hundred of hours/year providing quotes for tours?  E-tour, a PHP application provides instant quotes and reservations of tour services of travel agencies or tour operators. Easy to use and install. The script call for the registered user to select/enter requested information upon which online quote and by email or a reservation is generated. Requires PHP 5.2.4, Mysql, email server. Prices, services, categories, tour operators, languages, users and more settings are configurable. 

For limited time free installation and basic customization (template for reservation, taxes, etc.) Please visit our website and online demo!
